Gap Year

Preface

It has been almost nine months since my crash landing in Berlin, and finally I’ve carved out the time to commit some events to the record. With each passing day, I recall a little less. Thus, like many things in my life, these writings are motivated by fear.

There are many wild situations that I found myself in. I just don’t want to forget those. But more importantly, I want to both formalize and compress the updates that were made to my world model.

To the furthest degree possible, I will not indulge in hyperbole and where feasible will include first-hand accounts of others. I also am more than likely to express controversial opinions.

We will cover events from July 5, 2021 to October 14, 2022, with digressions outside of this range for context-building.

I’ve informally called this phase of my life, Gap Year. So I guess that’s the name here. We’ll see how it goes.
